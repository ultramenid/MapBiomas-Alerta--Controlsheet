Best Practices Applied:

  • Glass on KEY elements only - Stats cards and table container
  • Regular solid for everything else - Buttons, dropdowns, search box
  • Moderate blur - 6-8px (not 20px+)
  • Sufficient opacity - 60-65% white for readability
  • Subtle borders - 1px at 5-6% opacity
  • Sharp corners - 2px radius max
  • Text stays readable - High contrast on glass panels
  • Dark glass for emphasis - Total card stands out
  • Dot pattern visible - Background shows through at 25% opacity
  • Feature detection - Fallback for browsers without backdrop-filter

This is the proper way to do glassmorphism. Restrained, readable, professional. Does this work for you?
